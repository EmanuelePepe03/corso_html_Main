My story

My name is Emanuele Pepe, and I am 21 years old. I am currently a its student, working hard to pursue my degree in Full Stack Development. Ever since I was a child, I have been fascinated by technology, always eager to understand how things work and how I can create something new. This passion led me to choose my field of study, and so far, the journey has been both challenging and exciting.
Growing up in a small town, I always dreamed of exploring the world beyond my surroundings. Moving to a big city for university was a huge step for me, and it has opened my eyes to so many opportunities. I have met people from different cultures, learned new perspectives, and broadened my horizons. University life is not just about studying; it is also about personal growth and independence. Balancing my academic responsibilities with my social life has been a learning process. Some days are stressful, filled with assignments, deadlines, and exams. Other days are rewarding, especially when I successfully complete a project or grasp a complex concept. I enjoy coding, solving problems, and working on innovative ideas with my classmates. Being in this environment constantly pushes me to improve myself and stay motivated.
Outside of academics, I have a deep interest in playing video games and doing sports. Playing video games helps me relax after long study sessions, and I often engage in sports to clear my mind. These activities keep me grounded and help me manage stress effectively. As I continue my studies, my goal is to gain more practical experience through internships and personal projects. I aspire to become a skilled software developer, contributing to meaningful advancements in the tech industry. The road ahead is long, but I am excited about the journey and the possibilities that the future holds.
